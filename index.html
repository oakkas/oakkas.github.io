Dolly 2.0 is an open-source, instruction-following large language model (LLM) suitable for research and commercial use. The model is based on the EleutherAI pythia model family and has 12 billion parameters. Fine-tuned on a new human-generated dataset called databricks-dolly-15k. The dataset contains 15,000 high-quality prompt/response pairs. Designed specifically for instruction tuning in LLMs. Created by over 5,000 Databricks employees during March and April 2023. Open-sourcing Dolly 2.0 aims to address issues of bias, accountability, and AI safety. Encourages diverse stakeholders to contribute to AI technology. Allows organizations to own their models without sharing sensitive data with third parties. Dolly 2.0's release is expected to seed follow-on works and further advancements in LLMs. To get started with Dolly 2.0, visit the Databricks Hugging Face page and the Dolly repo on databricks-labs to download the model weights and databricks-dolly-15k dataset.
